Established 2024

Connecting Bangladesh to Global Opportunities

World Flight Travels is a government-approved overseas manpower recruitment agency headquartered in Rampura, Dhaka, Bangladesh.

We specialize in connecting Bangladeshi talent with reputable international employers across Asia, the Middle East, Europe, and beyond. At our heart lies a deep commitment to ethical recruitment and workforce excellence.

Govt. Approved Agency

Our Vision

To become a leading global recruitment agency recognized for integrity, professionalism, and excellence in delivering ethical manpower solutions.

Our Mission

  • Supply qualified, motivated, and reliable workers to international employers.
  • Maintain transparent and compliant recruitment practices.
  • Ensure candidate welfare and safety from selection to deployment.

Empowering People, Building Futures

At World Flight Travels, we believe every worker represents Bangladesh's pride and potential. Our mission is not just to send manpower abroad — it’s to transform lives and support families.
